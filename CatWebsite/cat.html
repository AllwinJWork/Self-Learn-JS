All About Cats

Cats are a popular household pet and are known for their playful and independent nature. They come in many different breeds and colors, each with their own unique characteristics.

Cat Facts

  1. Cats spend an average of 16 hours a day sleeping
  2. Cats have excellent night vision
  3. Cats are one of the few animals that can make their own vitamin C
  4. Cats can run up to 30 miles per hour
